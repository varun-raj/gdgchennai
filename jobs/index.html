We help the developers change the world

Google Technology User Groups (GDGs) are user groups for people who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android and App Engine platforms, to product APIs like the YouTube API and the Google Calendar API, to initiatives like OpenSocial. Check out code.google.com for the full list of offerings. GDG Chennai aims at bringing like minded people together and giving them a platform to develop, brainstorm and interact among yourselves.
